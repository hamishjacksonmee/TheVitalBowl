About the
Breakfast

The vital bowl creates delicious, satisfying, sugar-free muesli and chia seed puddings that contribute to a balanced diet and healthier you. Life is sweet enough without sugar and it’s all about finding vitality and balance. We believe in using top of the line, high-quality ingredients that have countless nutritional benefits. The Vital Bowl offers breakfasts, which showcase our belief in bringing food back to the basics and cutting out the unwanted sugar. Each bowl is made fresh with love from Auckland, New Zealand.

Crispy Coco—
Nut
Muesli

The Vital Bowl’s muesli is sugar-free, nourishing, and deliciously crunchy. All of the ingredients are superior in quality, high-end and have elite nutritional function. The shelves are packed with misleading and deceptive “healthy” breakfast cereals which are actually full of sugar. As a consequence, this limits your control over sugar intake, weight gain and overall health. The Vital Bowl provides genuine sugar-free muesli which gives you back the control and choice of your sugar intake.

Crispy Coco-Nut Muesli is available to buy in freshly packed, 5 serving bags from the market.

Chia Seed
Puddings

Chia seed puddings are a fabulous change from your typical breakfast foods. They pack a powerful nutritious punch that contributes to healthy and vital life.
