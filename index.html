About the Company

The vital bowl creates delicious, satisfying, sugar-free muesli and chia seed puddings that contribute to a balanced diet and healthier you. Life is sweet enough without sugar and it’s all about finding vitality and balance. We believe in using top of the line, high-quality ingredients that have countless nutritional benefits. The Vital Bowl offers breakfasts, which showcase our belief in bringing food back to the basics and cutting out the unwanted sugar. Each bowl is made fresh with love from Auckland, New Zealand.

Crispy Coco-Nut Muesli

The Vital Bowl’s muesli is sugar-free, nourishing, and deliciously crunchy. All of the ingredients are superior in quality, high-end and have elite nutritional function. The shelves are packed with misleading and deceptive “healthy” breakfast cereals which are actually full of sugar. As a consequence, this limits your control over sugar intake, weight gain and overall health. The Vital Bowl provides genuine sugar-free muesli which gives you back the control and choice of your sugar intake. You get to decide how you would like to sweeten and enjoy your breakfast.

Crispy Coco-Nut Muesli is available to buy in freshly packed, 5 serving bags from the market.
Ingredients: Wholegrain oats, coconut flakes, macadamia, almonds, cashews, hazelnuts, pumpkin seeds, chia seeds, cinnamon and coconut oil.

Chia Seed Puddings

Chia seed puddings are a fabulous change from your typical breakfast foods. They pack a powerful nutritious punch that contributes to healthy and vital life. Each pudding is made with top end, high-quality ingredients which create a tasty and nourishing breakfast. Every tiny chia seed is loaded with antioxidants, omega 3, fibre, low G.I and protein.

Blueberry Coconut Chia Pudding (title and pic)- Sugar-free, gluten free, and dairy free

Ingredients: Unsweetened coconut milk, black chia seeds, organic freeze-dried blueberry powder, stevia and topped with coconut yoghurt, coconut flakes and fresh blueberries.

Peanut Choc Chia Pudding (title and pic)- Sugar-free and gluten free

Ingredients: Unsweetened vanilla almond milk, unsweetened coconut milk, black chia seeds, 100% natural peanut butter, organic cacao powder, rice malt syrup (fructose free sweetener), stevia and topped with Greek yoghurt, fresh banana slices and cacao nibs.
